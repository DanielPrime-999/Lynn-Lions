About the Team

The lynn Lions are a highly powerful, competitive esports team that is based in the county of Norfolk. their top players consist of 6 people (and one of their pets), who will face any challenges that is thrown at them. they have faced highly skilled players from across the county, country and globe. their journey started off as a county wide esports competition against Norfolks top teams: Swaffham Swifts, Dersingham Dynamo and Cromer Crabs
